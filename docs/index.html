Fortune 500 by the Numbers

Using real SEC EDGAR 10-K filing data, this project analyzes 12 Fortune 500 companies across 5 sectors. Combined 2023 revenue: $3.7T. Walmart leads by revenue at $611B but Microsoft wins on margins at 34.2%. Apple generates $100B in free cash flow — more than the entire energy sector combined. Amazon grew fastest at +19.6% CAGR since 2019. Chevron was hit hardest by COVID: -35.4% revenue in 2020.
